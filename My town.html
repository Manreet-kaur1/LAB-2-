My town is SHRI MUKTSAR SAHIB

Sri Muktsar Sahib (often referred to as Muktsar) is a historical city and district headquarters in Punjab, India.

The modern day Sri Muktsar Sahib city was historically a semi-desert terrain named Khidrana or Khidrane de dhab, situated near a lake.
